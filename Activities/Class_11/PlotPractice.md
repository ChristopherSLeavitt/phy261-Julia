## Activity 4

### Creating a 2x2 plot grid

In Julia make a 2x2 grid plot. The individual plots should be:

Top Left: $y = 2\sin(2\pi x),$ from $x=0$ to $x=2\,\mathrm{s}$. 

Bottom Left: $y = 2e^{-x}\sin(2\pi x),$ from $x=0$ to $x=2\,\mathrm{s}$. 

Top right: $ k = 4\sin^2(2\pi t)$ from $t=0$ to $t=4\,\mathrm{s}$.

Bottom right: $-sin\theta$ vs $\cos\theta$ for $\theta=0$ to $\theta = 2\pi$.
